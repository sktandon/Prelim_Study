A whole new game!


Now we're going to play a quick multiple choice paper folding game. In this game, you are going to imagine a piece of paper being folded, hole punched, and unfolded. There are figures drawn above a horizontal line and some beneath. The figures above show a square paper being folded, and the last of these has one or two small circles drawn to show where the paper has been punched. Each hole is punched all the way through the thicknesses of the paper. One of the five figures below the line shows where the holes will be when the paper is completely unfolded. You are to pick which one of these figures is correct and click on that figure.

Try the example below - in this one, only one hole was punched through the folded paper. In all the questions the folds are made above the line and the paper is not moved in any other way. Remember, the answer is the figure that shows where the holes will be when the paper is unfolded.

It's better to take your time and be sure of your answer before moving on. You'll play the game for only three minutes!


The correct answer above is the third paper. Please select the third piece of paper here.

After you've selected the one you think is right, click Next to record your answer and move to the next question.

When you're ready to begin the game, press next!
